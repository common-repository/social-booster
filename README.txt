== Description ==
Automate social sharing of your website posts using Social Booster.

Using this plugin, you can automatically share your blog posts, landing pages, infographics, WooCommerce store products, etc on all of your social channels without leaving your website dashboard. 

Easily auto post on Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, LinkedIn and many more with just a few clicks.

No need to log in and manually post on every social profile.

Share your posts on social media from the post edit page or on Social Booster dashboard.

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YyMmPzRpG6E]

You can simply choose the profiles you wish to share on, set caption, watch preview, and schedule or instantly share the content on all of your social media profiles within minutes.

Add your social accounts to Social Booster once, and you no longer need to visit every profile for sharing again.

== Why Use Social Booster? ==

Social Booster will be your personal companion to automate social sharing for maximum engagement.

It is simple and straight forward - you focus on creating your website post/content and automatically post on social media using Social booster.

**Instant share and save time**
Once you publish a post or a page, you can directly share it on your social sites from the post edit mode using Social Booster.

Simply scroll down to find the social booster section. Here, you can choose your desired social channels, set an engaging caption, and share instantly with just a single click.

>No need to waste several minutes by visiting every social media profile to share your website posts.

**Schedule recurring post share**
Do you have a post or page you want to share regularly on social media?

Simply schedule it to be shared daily, weekly or monthly for automatic social posting.

Once you schedule a post to be shared, leave the rest to Social Booster. You can work with other content while your post will be automatically re-shared according to your set intervals.

**Get maximum traffic on active hours**
You can also schedule your social post for a specific date and time.

It is important to share your website posts on active hours to reach more prospects. But you might get busy during those hours.

No problem. Simply schedule a social auto post with your desired date and time. You can then focus on other activities while Social Booster will automatically share your post on social media at the best hours.

Thus you can expect maximum engagement.

**Revive Your Old Blog Posts**
Choose any of your old posts that deserve to be shared again on your social media. You may browse all of your blog posts on Social Booster dashboard. 
 
Simply choose the one you want to auto post on social media and instant share or schedule social share with a few clicks.

**No Sign of Automation Tool**
Unlike other social automation tools, Social Booster leaves no trace of plugin use. You will enjoy complete white label posting.

>Social Booster will not leave any extra messages such as 'Posted with Social Booster' or any such message.

Your social posts will look genuine as if you personally shared it.

**Social Auto Post Calendar**
You can manage all your scheduled social posts or trace all your auto post history through an interactive calendar.

You can check date and time, and re-schedule posts as you wish directly from the calendar.

Appsero SDK **does not gather any data by default.** The SDK only starts gathering basic telemetry data **when a user allows it via the admin notice**. We collect the data to ensure great user experience for all our users.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Installation Instruction =
1. Download the latest version of the Social Media Auto Publish and Schedule Plugin - Social Booster Plugin to your computer from here.
1. With an FTP program, access your sites server.
1. Upload (copy) the Plugin file(s) or folder to the /wp-content/plugins folder.
1. In your WordPress Administration Panels, click on Plugins from the menu.
1. You should see Social Booster Plugin listed. If not, with your FTP program, check the folder to see if it is installed. If it isn't, upload the file(s) again. If it is, delete the files and upload them again.
1. To turn the Social Media Auto Publish and Schedule Plugin - Social Booster Plugin on, click Activate.

= Alternatively you can also follow the following steps to install the plugin =
1. In your WordPress Administration Panels, click on Add New option under Plugins from the menu.
1. Click on upload at the top.
1. Browse the location and select the Social_Booster and click install now.
1. To turn the Social Media Auto Publish and Schedule Plugin - Social Booster Plugin on, click Activate.
1. Check your WordPress blog to see if the Plugin is working.

= Can I add multiple social accounts? =
Yes. You can connect multiple social accounts.

= How to schedule recurring social share? =
On your post edit page, scroll down to Social Booster section. Then chose Schedule post. Now select daily, weekly or monthly to set recurring social post of the content you are editing.

= How to schedule on specific time? =
To be able to schedule on specific time, you first need to upgrade to Pro. Then, when Scheduling a post for social share, you will get the option to set a date and time to set up the schedule.

= How can I change/edit my scheduled social posts? =
On your post edit page, scroll down to Social Booster section. Then chose Re-schedule post. Here you will see a list of all the posts that you have scheduled for social sharing. Edit the one you want to change schedule for here.

= Do you save my passwords =
No. In Social Booster, we use the oAuth system through your social media APIs. So we have no access to your username or your password.

= Why do I create apps on Social channels? =
You need to create apps for two important reasons:
1. For security reasons. You will give your social channels authorization, not us, so you won’t have to share your password to us.
2. Our plugin leaves no trace of plugin use because it is processed through your social channels’ apps.
